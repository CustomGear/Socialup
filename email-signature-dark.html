How to use:
Click inside the dark box → Select All (⌘A) → Copy (⌘C) → open Gmail / Apple Mail signature settings → paste (⌘V).

Note: Dark backgrounds render best in Gmail, Apple Mail, and modern clients. Outlook on Windows may show a white background behind the text.
