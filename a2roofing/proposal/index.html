Phase One

Website Rebuild & Marketing Foundation

The current website would be rebuilt and expanded using the existing A2 Roofing branding, colours, photography and overall visual direction. The new website would include multiple pages designed around the specific services and geographic areas A2 Roofing wants to target.

This would include:

  • Professionally rebuilt website
  • Dedicated pages for each priority roofing service
  • Dedicated pages for priority cities and service areas
  • English and French website content
  • Project galleries and completed project pages
  • Before-and-after roofing examples
  • Customer reviews and testimonials
  • Warranty, insurance and company trust information
  • Mobile-focused calls to action
  • Dedicated Google Ads landing pages
  • Search-engine-friendly website structure
  • Technical SEO and structured data
  • Google Analytics and Search Console setup
  • Call and form conversion tracking
  • Google Business Profile creation and optimization
  • Website launch, redirects and indexing support
Included in Phase One

Interactive estimate form

The website would include an interactive, step-by-step estimate form designed to make the inquiry process easier and more engaging. Instead of asking customers to complete a long traditional form, the form would guide them through a short series of questions, such as:

Type of roofing service required Property type Approximate roof size Current roofing material Project urgency Property location Preferred method of contact Photos of the existing roof

The form can change based on the customer's selections, helping A2 Roofing collect better information before contacting the lead. The objective is to improve the customer experience, increase form completion rates and provide the sales team with more qualified project information.

Included in Phase One

Lead tracking

Every major lead source would be tracked so A2 Roofing can understand which marketing activities are producing real opportunities. Tracking would include:

  • Phone calls
  • Estimate requests
  • Website form submissions
  • Google Ads leads
  • Google Business Profile activity
  • Organic Google traffic
  • Social media traffic
  • Landing-page conversions
  • Cost per advertising lead

The focus would not simply be on increasing website traffic. The focus would be on generating and tracking qualified roofing opportunities.

What to expect

Expected timeline

A2 Roofing should expect approximately three months before meaningful progress becomes visible.

Google Ads can begin generating website traffic and inquiries shortly after launch. However, the first several weeks are also used to collect data, identify the strongest searches and eliminate keywords that produce poor-quality traffic. Organic Google rankings, Google Maps visibility and AI search visibility require more time. The first three months should be viewed as the foundation and data-collection stage.

More competitive searches may take longer, depending on

The targeted city or region The competitiveness of the roofing service The number and quality of Google reviews The amount of project content available The authority of competing roofing companies The consistency of ongoing marketing work

Progress would be measured through

Qualified phone calls Estimate requests Form submissions Advertising cost per lead Website conversion rate Google Business Profile activity Organic search visibility Keyword movement Lead quality

Specific rankings or lead volumes cannot be guaranteed. The strategy would focus on building a measurable and sustainable marketing system that improves over time.

Before we begin

Access required to begin

To begin the website, Google Business Profile and marketing setup, access to the following accounts and information would be required.

Website & domain access

  • Current website administrator access
  • Current website hosting access
  • Domain registrar or DNS access
  • Access to any website forms or email delivery systems
  • Access to the current website database or files, if available

Administrator or collaborator access is preferred instead of sharing personal passwords whenever possible.

Google access

  • Google Business Profile access, if a profile already exists
  • Google Ads access, if an account already exists
  • Google Analytics access
  • Google Search Console access
  • Google Tag Manager access, if currently installed
  • Access to the Google account that will own the business profile and advertising account

If these accounts don't currently exist, they can be created during setup. The business owner may need to complete certain Google verification steps directly, including video, phone, email or mailed verification.

A2 Roofing would be responsible for adding its own payment method directly to the Google Ads account. The advertising budget would be charged by Google and would not pass through SocialUp.

Lead management

Before launching campaigns, A2 Roofing should identify:

  • Who receives new leads
  • Which phone number should be promoted
  • Which email address receives estimate requests
  • How quickly leads are normally contacted
  • Which services are most profitable
  • Which locations are preferred
  • Which jobs should not be advertised
  • How completed jobs and revenue will be recorded

Tracking works best when leads can be followed from the initial phone call or form submission through to the final estimate and completed roofing project.
